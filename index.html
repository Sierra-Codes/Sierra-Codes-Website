About

Sierra Codes was founded by Stella Bullo, a linguist turned developer (PhD in Linguistics). We work where language, health and technology meet — building small, humane tools that turn lived experience into practical applications.

You can also explore Stella’s writing for reflections on language, code, and applied research.
