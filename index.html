About

Sierra Codes was founded by Stella Bullo, a former academic with a PhD in Linguistics turned developer. We work where language, health and technology meet, building small, humane tools that turn lived experience into practical applications.

You can also explore Stella’s writing for reflections on language, code, and applied research.
