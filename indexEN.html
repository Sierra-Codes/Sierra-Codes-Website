About

I’m Stella Bullo, a linguist turned developer.
After a PhD and academic career in the UK researching linguistics and health communication, I moved into coding and NLP to build tools that make language data usable, ethical, and human-centred.
Through Sierra Codes, I design bilingual NLP and annotation workflows, and develop lightweight front-end solutions for local professionals and small businesses.
